Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This policy is GDPR compliant.

1. Introduction

TooltipAI ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

This policy applies to all users of the Service, including those in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

TooltipAI

Email: DixStdGdl@hotmail.com

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name (optional)
  • Password (stored as a bcrypt hash — we cannot read your password)

3.2 UIA Element Data

When you hover over UI elements, TooltipAI reads and transmits the following data to generate AI explanations:

  • Element name (e.g., "Threshold", "Save Button")
  • Element type and role (e.g., "Slider", "Button")
  • Element value (when available)
  • Parent application name

We do NOT collect: Screenshots, file contents, browsing history, keystrokes, clipboard data, or any personal files.

3.3 Usage Data

We may collect anonymized usage statistics, including:

  • Features used (tooltip categories, bookmark usage)
  • General geographic region (country level only)
  • App version and operating system version

3.4 Payment Data

Payment information is processed by LemonSqueezy and is never stored on our servers. We receive only a transaction ID and subscription status from LemonSqueezy.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To generate AI-powered tooltips and provide core functionality
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account and subscription
  • Communication: To respond to support requests and send essential service notifications
  • Improvement: To analyze anonymized usage patterns and improve the Software
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, we process your data based on the following legal grounds:

Consent (Article 6(1)(a))
When you create an account and agree to this Privacy Policy.
Contract Performance (Article 6(1)(b))
To provide the Service you've subscribed to and manage your account.
Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f))
To improve the Software, ensure security, and communicate with you about service updates.

6. Data Sharing

We share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • OpenAI API: UIA element data is sent to OpenAI for AI-powered tooltip generation. OpenAI's privacy policy governs their handling of this data. View OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • LemonSqueezy: Payment processing. LemonSqueezy handles payment data under their own privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or governmental request.

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Data Retention

● Account data: Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account closure.
● UIA element data: Not stored — processed in real-time and immediately discarded.
● Bookmarks & history: Stored locally on your device. Pro plan syncs to our servers encrypted at rest; deleted upon account closure.
● Anonymized usage data: Retained indefinitely in aggregate form (cannot be traced to individuals).

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Bcrypt hashing for passwords (with salt)
  • JWT tokens with short expiration times
  • Regular security audits and dependency updates
  • Microsoft Azure hosting with SOC 2 compliance

No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by the GDPR.

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under data protection law:

Right of Access (Article 15)
Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
Right to Rectification (Article 16)
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
Right to Erasure (Article 17)
Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
Right to Restriction (Article 18)
Request restriction of processing of your personal data.
Right to Data Portability (Article 20)
Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Right to Object (Article 21)
Object to processing based on legitimate interests, including direct marketing.
Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at DixStdGdl@hotmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information, so there is nothing to opt out of.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

11. Cookies & Tracking

The TooltipAI desktop application does not use cookies. Our website (tooltip-ai-landing) may use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics that profile individual users.

12. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

13. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States where our servers are hosted (Microsoft Azure). We ensure that international data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and, where appropriate, by email. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.

15. Contact & Data Protection Officer

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, contact us at:

TooltipAI

Email: DixStdGdl@hotmail.com

If you are in the EEA and believe we have not addressed your concern, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
